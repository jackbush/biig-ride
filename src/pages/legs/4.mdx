**Visa:** 90 days visa-free. **Non-Schengen.**

## Route: Northern Albanian Alps Loop

1. **Entry from Greece → Shkodër area**
   - Via Kakavija border or Krystallopigi/Kapshticë crossing

2. **Shkodër → Theth** (2–3 days)
   - The climb from Shkodër to Theth is the signature Albanian bikepacking experience
   - Paved for first 20 km, then gravel/mud. 2.2"+ tires essential.
   - Theth: dramatic limestone valley, waterfall, lock-in tower (blood feud history)

3. **Theth → Valbona** (1–2 days)
   - Via Valbona Pass (1,795 m) — classic Peaks of the Balkans hiking trail
   - Hike-a-bike over the pass, rideable sections either side
   - Valbona Valley: ancient beech forests (UNESCO), crystal-clear river

4. **Lake Koman Ferry** (1 day)
   - Ride to the ferry at Fierze, take the boat through the flooded canyon
   - One of Europe's most scenic boat rides
   - Emerge at Koman, ride to Shkodër

5. **Shkodër → Montenegro** (1 day)
   - Border crossing at Hani i Hotit or Muriqan

## Key Logistics

- **Budget:** €10–15/day. Guesthouse + dinner = €15. Raki is offered freely.
- **Stray dogs:** More aggressive than anywhere else on this route. Carry stones. A walking stick helps.
- **Roads:** Expect rough gravel, mud, and stream crossings in the north. Not technical — just rugged.

## Nature & Drawing Highlights

Albanian Alps are karst limestone — sheer white peaks, turquoise rivers, endemic Balkan flora. Ophiolite belt (exposed ocean crust rock) in central Albania — geologically rare and visually striking (dark serpentinite among white limestone).

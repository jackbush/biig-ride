**Visa:** 90 days visa-free. **Non-Schengen.**

## Route: Mountains to Coast

1. **Enter from Albania** near Shkodër → ride north through Prokletije (Accursed Mountains, Montenegro side)

2. **Durmitor National Park** (3–4 days)
   - Limestone massif, glacial lakes (Black Lake is stunning), dense forest
   - Excellent dirt roads and 4×4 tracks through the park
   - Wild camping at altitude is the way to do this
   - **Tara River Canyon:** deepest in Europe (1,300 m). Ride the rim road.

3. **South to the coast** (2–3 days)
   - Via Lovćen National Park (hairpin descent to Kotor Bay)
   - Or more directly to Bar for the ferry

## Ferry

**Bar → Bari (Italy).** Overnight, ~9 hrs. ~€40–60 with bike. Book a few days ahead in August (busy season).

## Nature

Old-growth black pine forests, griffon vultures overhead. Bears and wolves present but extremely shy — not a real concern. Standard food-hanging practice is sufficient.

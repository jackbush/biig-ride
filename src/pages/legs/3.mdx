**Border:** İpsala (Turkey) → Kipi (Greece). Straightforward for bikes.

**Schengen days used: 3**

## Route

Kipi → ride west through Thrace → cross into Albania at Kakavija or Krystallopigi.

~300–400 km of flat-to-rolling agricultural land. Ride it fast — every day costs a Schengen day. The interesting mountains (Pindus, Zagori) are worth it but cost extra days you can't spare.

**Alternative:** If time pressure, bus from the border area to Thessaloniki or Ioannina, then ride to Albania.

## Notes

The point of this leg is speed. Thrace is flat, agricultural, and unremarkable. The goal is to bank days for the Alps later in the trip.

Keep food and accommodation simple. Push the daily distance. Albania is just across the border and the clock stops ticking the moment you cross.
